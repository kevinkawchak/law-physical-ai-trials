\section{The solution: a new section 515D}
\label{sec:b}

The bill adds section 515D to the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (21 U.S.C. \S~360e-5): no robot-patient interaction code may be generated or executed unless an automated verification, validation, and uncertainty quantification gate has first cleared, with the record documented and attested. A record made after the fact does not satisfy the section.
