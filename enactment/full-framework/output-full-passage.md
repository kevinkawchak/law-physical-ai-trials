## output-full-passage

This file records the markdown narrative of Stage 3, the full-framework. It is not
the source files; those are `main.tex`, `passagestyle.sty`, `references.bib`, and the
twelve `sections/*.tex`.

### What the full stage produced

Every bracketed `[DRAFTING INSTRUCTIONS: ...]` left in the draft was replaced with
finished, publication-grade prose drawn from the exact sources it named. The
manuscript now reads as a senior policy author wrote it, defines each term before
first substantive use, and gains technical substance as it proceeds. No scaffolding
bracket remains.

### How the eight questions are answered

- Mandate: three named gaps in existing law (embodiment and sequencing, financial
  data, cost asymmetry), anchored to the record on preventable medical error.
- Authority: a commerce-power amendment to the FD\&C Act adding section 515D, with a
  rule of construction and a State savings clause that preserve existing law.
- Safety: verification before generation, the ten-gate schedule bound to consensus
  standards, and the three hard catastrophe predicates.
- Fiscal: the 58 million dollar authorization, no net mandatory spending, and the
  roughly nineteen-to-one cost reduction over conventional review.
- Constituents: the platform's rollout to 50 through 100 sites and the benefit to
  rural and underserved districts.
- Bipartisanship: one provision that a deregulation-minded and a safety-minded member
  can each support on their own terms.
- Coalition: clinicians, patients, industry, and the States, with every standard
  objection routed to a cited answer.
- Passage path: ordinary regular order in both chambers, known vote thresholds, a
  defined implementation runway, and a platform already shown to work.

### Figures and tables

The manuscript carries all twenty colored TikZ figures (nine in the body, eleven in
the appendix compendium) in the strict palette, each with a uniform
`\vspace{-0.75cm}` figure-to-caption gap, and six body-width tables with columns
sized to the text each column holds. The verify stage audits every figure and table
before the final stage applies publication polish.
